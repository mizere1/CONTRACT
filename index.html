📋 Learning Guidelines

🎯 Course Objectives
This comprehensive course covers the fundamentals of Contract Law with special focus on Remedies for Breach and Damages. By completing this course, you will understand:
• The essential elements of contract formation
• Terms and conditions in contracts
• Remedies available for breach of contract
• Calculation and award of damages
• Defenses against breach claims
⏱️ How to Use This App
1. Select a Lesson: Click on any lesson card to start learning
2. Listen Actively: Use the audio player to listen to the complete lesson
3. Review Captions: Read along with the provided captions and notes
4. Mark as Complete: Click the "Mark as Complete" button when finished
5. Track Progress: Monitor your learning progress in the Progress tab
💡 Key Concepts
Contract Formation: Agreement, consideration, intention to create legal relations
Terms of Contract: Conditions, warranties, innominate terms
Breach of Contract: Failure to perform contractual obligations
Damages: Monetary compensation for breach
Remedies: Legal solutions available to the injured party
📚 Study Tips
• Listen to lessons in a quiet environment
• Take notes while listening
• Review captions multiple times
• Practice applying concepts to real scenarios
• Complete all lessons before assessment
• Revisit difficult topics as needed
🌍 Malawian Law Context
This course includes specific references to Malawian legal framework and how contract law principles apply in the Malawian context. Understanding these context-specific applications is crucial for legal practitioners in Malawi.
